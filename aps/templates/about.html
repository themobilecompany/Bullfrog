Attention Points

This Attention Point management system is based on The Mobile Company's Attention Points System Policy and implementation.

With Attention Points, Lead Links can put more/less relative focus on certain roles/sub-circles.
A fulltime 40 hour week roughly translates to 100 attention points (i.e. 1 full time equivalent). Or, 1 hours equals 2.5 Attention Points.
Assigned Attention Points should be seen as a maximum allowed focus. Therefore your total assigned Attention Points can add up to more than 100%.
Note: Attention Points are meant to be relative; Look at all your Roles and divide your focus relative to the assigned attention points.

For more information, please consult the documentation or your Attention Points Policy.
